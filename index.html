We are Homeless Hounds.

For over 15 years, Homeless Hounds has rescued thousands of domestic animals from abuse, neglect and euthanasia.

By rehabilitating animals in safe, caring environments, we've been able to rehabilitate sick or scared animals and put them into loving homes.

Join our Foster Carer network

Foster carers are the backbone of our organisation. Without homes to foster animals in, we would have no way of caring for them.

Caring for animals that have been through the worst that life can give them, and watching them come out as healthy, happy creatures is an incredibly rewarding experience.

By simply bringing an animal into your home for a couple weeks, you can literally make the difference between life and death for an innocent being.
